OUR MISSION STATEMENT:

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY FOR EVERY STUDENT

The sparks foundation is working to bring parity in education, making sure children have equal opportunity at success, irrespective of the financial background. To inspire students, help them innovate and let them integrate to build the next generation humankind.

The Sparks Foundation

Our Vision Statement -->
A world of enabled and connected little minds, building future.

Our Mission Statement -->
Inspire :- To inspire, motivate and encourage students to learn, create and help build a better society.
Innovate :- To teach new ways of thinking, to innovate and solve the problems on their own.
Integrate :- To let the students integrate and help each other, learn from each other and do well together.
